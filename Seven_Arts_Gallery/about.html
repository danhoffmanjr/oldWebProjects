More about The Seven Arts Gallery

The Seven Arts Gallery is a father and daughter team incorporating over 40 years of cultural and educational experience in the collecting business.

The President, Andrea Levy, graduated from Parsons School of Design with a Bachelors Degree in Communication Design. She then went into the publishing business, designing childrens books for Simon & Schuster in New York. After moving back to home base from the big city, she then worked in various design jobs eventually becoming a full time Art and Design teacher for a local College in Delaware. She is now a full time Masters student attending University of the Arts in Philadelphia. She is studying Book Art and Printmaking. She has traveled extensively throughout the world, from Africa to Barbados. She has lived and studied art in Europe, mainly in Paris.

The Vice President, Allan Wolfe Levy, is a recently retired Medical Physician who has long been a collector in history and the arts. His specialty was Anatomic and Clinical Pathology, spending the last 7 years of his medical career in Primary Care. He too has traveled extensively in Europe, spending long intervals of time in France, Great Britain, Italy, and the Middle East. His interests and expertise include historical, scientific and medical memorabilia.

Andrea's art background and Allan's scientific background provide a wonderful combination of knowledge for our company!
