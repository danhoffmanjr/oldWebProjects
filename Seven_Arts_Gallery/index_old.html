Welcome to The Seven Arts Gallery!

The Seven Arts Gallery is a fine art gallery and a collector's gallery. Our galleries, categorized on the left, contain objects which are creative, interesting, and historical. We sell mainly fine art and autographed or signed documents from many different genres of history. We specialize in lithographs and high quality collectible original art. Our inventory of historical documents and letters includes presidents, military personalities, artists, scientists and writers. People seeking out of the ordinary gifts or the serious collector will find us unique, professional, and gratifying!

We are a family business, carrying on the tradition of our relative, the famous Art Dealer and Theater personality, Anna Sosenko. Anna began collecting letters and art in the mid 1900's in New York and throughout Europe. She continued selling and collecting until her death in 2000.
